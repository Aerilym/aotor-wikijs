3 ABY

The Auditors of the Outer Rim Campaign stars here

The following events took place in the year 3 ABY, also known as 3280 LY according to the Lothal Calendar and year 7980 in the C.R.C. calendar.

Star Wars Canon Events

 

Month 1

Formed on Mos Shuuta, Tatooine in Arkanis Sector in the Outer Rim.

1 - Mos Shuuta: Escaped Teemos palace and stole the Krayt Fang from Mos Shuuta. They also steal/liberate R5-ST.

Travelled from Mos Shuuta, Tatooine to Nabat Spaceport, Ryloth.

2 - Nabat Spaceport: Found B'ura Ban onboard their stolen ship and took him back to Ryloth to meet with Nyn Kablo.

Travelled from Nabat to New Meen.

3 - New Meen: Travelled to the New Meen settlement with B'ura Ban on his speeder, fighting off an ambush and arriving in the evening.

4 - New Meen: Look around New Meen and investigate the thug mining operation, running over some thugs with B'ura Bann's speeder at night.

5 - New Meen: Raid on the thug mining base followed by a wild party in the thug's half-destroyed bar/base.

6 - Nabat Spaceport: Return to Nabat to find a Kubaz observing them. They then shop and talk to Nyn where they meet Ota who then employs them.

Travelled from Nabat, Ryloth to Gogum Hive, Geonosian Moon.

7 - Gogum Hive: Spice run to Roon, then to a geonotian moon to talk to some dukes, they then party with the duke's criminals he invited and meet new people.  They also buy some geonotian rifles for Nyn.

Travelled from Gogum Hive to Trellik Hive.

8 - Trellik Hive: Meeting with second duke, who they convince to let them sneak on a shipment to Teemos palace, with Orpa and Wex.

Travelled from Trellik Hive, Genonsian Moon to Mos Shuuta, Tatooine.

9 - Mos Shuuta: Sneak into Teemos palace, with R5 on standby with their ship. They steal Teemos data, fight an imperial vigil corvette, annoying a nemesis officer and getting the data into the hands of Jabba. Narrowly escaping and landing on Ryloth.

Travelled from Mos Shuuta, Tatooine to Nabat Spaceport, Ryloth.

10-35 - Nabat Spaceport: Repair ship, do some shopping, look into the bounty hunter Thweek, and rest.

 

Month 2

1 -  Nabat Spaceport: Information drop for Nyn at an imperial friendly bar at Nabat.

2 - Nabat Spaceport: With the ship repairs complete they accept some jobs to run guns and stew to The Wheel.

Travelled from Nabat, Ryloth to The Wheel, Malrood Sector in the Mid Rim.

33 - The Wheel: Coming out of hyperspace, they bump into a hyperspace pod, collecting it and heading to the station.

34 - The Wheel: Deliver Weapons and stew, and ask about Ropok.

Travelled from The Wheel to Rendili Outpost.

35 - Rendili Outpost: Ota contacts the group about an undercover mission to investigate corporate espionage near the wheel, the group travel there and investigates.

 

Month 3

1 - Rendili Outpost: They solve the mystery and in doing so encounter their imperial officer nemesis' sister and listen in on an imperial call, then return to the wheel.

Travelled from Rendili Outpost to The Wheel.

2 - The Wheel: Asking around about Reom and Barratech, meeting an information broker at a cantina, and a droid mechanic/barber.

3 - The Wheel: The party slice the hyperspace pod learning more about it and gaining the hyperspace jump vector.

4 - The Wheel: They buy 2 GONK droids and learn a lot about hyperspace pods and the wheel.

5 - The Wheel: They talk more to the information broker, asking about jobs and Baratech. They then find a job with Baratech and do some work for them.

6 - The Wheel: Under the employment of Baratech two members of the group investigate their own ship as a source of a transponder signal, faking it to their employer.

7 - The Wheel: Looking for another job and to leave the wheel, the party smuggle weapons for Ota and the Droid Tech/Barber Grotto, taking them along the Perlimian Trade Route to Corellia.

Travelled from The Wheel to Cornet City, Corellia in the Core Worlds.

15 - Cornet City: The party arrive at Cornet City, and in a routine inspection, an imperial inspection officer finds the hidden compartment, he is bribed to not reveal the information under the guise of a fee. They then travel to a droid shop using spuber, where they pay for upgrades to R5, making him an astromech, who they can pick up in 2 days. They then travel to Servals Space (A bar) to meet with their contact, who provides them with information about a heist on the Black Sun, and provides them with some tools.

16 - Cornet City: The party gather surveillance on the building and come up with a plan, they then enter the building and steal the data, trapping many guards in lifts.

17 - Cornet City: Pick up R5 and Leave Corellia to follow and raid a ship.

Travelled from Cornet City, Corellia to Space.

18 - Space: The ship is overcome and taken over during a joint hyperspace flight away from corellia

Travelled from Space to Cornet City, Corellia.

19 - Saved Serval and left Corellia back to The Wheel.

Travelled from Cornet City, Corellia to The Wheel, Malrood Sector in the Mid Rim.

34 - The Wheel: Looked around the wheel and met with Dash Corrik. They then looked for jobs they could do, settling on a passenger run to Chad and a cargo run to PLACE with PEOPLE WHO WENT.

Travelled from The Wheel to Chad in the Outer Rim.

35 - Chad: Brought Aleraanian passengers to chad and buried a box from dach

 

Month 4

Travelled from Chad to The Wheel in the Mid Rim.

2 - The Wheel: Meeting with Dash.

3-5 - The Wheel: Survalence of Reom.

6 - The Wheel: Finish cargo run and planning.

7 - The Wheel: Abduct Reom and leave the wheel, they questioned him over the course of a few days as they headed for Cholganna.

Travelled from The Wheel to Cholganna in the Outer Rim.

14 - Cholganna: Search the planet and land, fighting bugs.

15 - Cholganna: Find ship engine section and main body.

16 - Cholganna: Travel to the Retreat and fight the empire, escaping with the survivors.

Travelled from Cholganna to Raxus Prime, Raxus in the Outer Rim.

18-20 - Raxus Prime: Repair ship and trade with Jawas.

Travelled from Raxus Prime, Raxus to The Wheel in the Mid Rim.

23 - The Wheel: Dropped off survivors and boarded a passenger ship to Rodia.

Travel from The Wheel in the Mid Rim to Karsteeku, Rodia, SECTOR in the Outer Rim.

27 - Space: Encountered a distress signal and responded, saving 2 people. Continued to Rodia.

32 - Karsteeku: Got ship from contact and prepared for the mission.

Travelled from Rodia to Deep Space in SECTOR.

35 - Space: Took over the ship and proceeded to Nabat Spaceport, Ryloth.

Travelled from Seep Space to Nabat, Ryloth, Gaulus Sector in the Outer Rim
