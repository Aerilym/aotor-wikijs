The Auditors first visited New Meen after their escape from Tatooine

 

New Meen is a seedy little town on the planet Ryloth, near ryll mines. Angu Drombb, one of Teemo's henchmen, tried to frighten the inhabitants to gain control of the ryll spice trade. The Auditors put a stop to Drombb and Teemo's plans and liberated the small town.
