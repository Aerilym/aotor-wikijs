The Auditors first visited Ryloth after their escape from Tatooine

Ryloth is a planet in the Gaulus Sector of the Outer Rim Territories, and the homeworld of the Twi'lek species. Its terrain is varied, as the surface is filled with jungles, mesas, valleys, and volcanoes. A forest covers its equator, filled with dangerous predators. Given the varied and dangerous terrain, Twi'leks typically in caves and dug out areas underground where it was safer.
