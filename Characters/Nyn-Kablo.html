Nyn Kablo is a female Twi'lek militant from the planet Ryloth. She's a leader of a rebel cell that was formed to protect the interests of the common Twi'leks on Ryloth and came into conflict with a group of thugs led by Angu Drombb that were terrorizing the nearby mine of New Meen. Her group was headquartered in the town of Nabat.

Nyn runs the Ryloth Resistance in Nabat and funds it with contract jobs and ryll spice shipments.
