Saved by the Auditors

 

B'ura Ban is a male Twi'lek militant from the planet Ryloth. Travelling from the village of New Meen to the city of Nabat, B'ura Ban was attacked by a gang of thugs in the employ of Teemo the Hutt. During the fighting, he lost one of his lekku to the Trandoshan bounty hunter Trex, who subsequently imprisoned B'ura Ban aboard his YT-1300 freighter, the Krayt Fang. B'ura Ban was later released and returned to his homeworld by The Auditors.
